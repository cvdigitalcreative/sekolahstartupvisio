About Visio

Visio is one of tech start-up incubator in Indonesia to help a tech startup founders can compete with others and launch their products to market. We have a purpose to build a strong tech startup ecosystem in Indonesia. Since 2017, we had developed 55 tech start-ups and 192 startup founders.

What is Sekolah Startup Visio

Sekolah Startup Visio is an event held by Visio Incubator to embrace the people who have not known yet about tech startup and don't have any background of tech education but want to build a business in a tech startup.

Mentors

Peter Shearer

Peter was the Chief Business Development Officer of WIR group; the leading brand-led innovative, technology & entertainment group of companies in Asia. He served as the senior leader in developing various new businesses and oversaw the entire development of new media, gaming products, and services, as well as leading strategic planning and guiding all marketing activities. Fortune Magazine listed him as one of the Top 40 Fortune under 40. Peter has appeared in the Forbes Magazine, Fortune Magazine, Bisnis Indonesia Newspaper, Bloomberg TV, Kompas TV, Metro TV, Berita Satu, and many others. Now, Peter is pursuing his deep passion as a social entrepreneur to help the micro enterprises reaching a better life. He believes that if the small society reaches a certain level of prosperity, Indonesia will grow better. With that, he founded his own company named Wahyoo.

Mentors

Yopie Suryadi

Yopie Suryadi is the co-founder of Mtarget. It has helped a lot of marketing in many companies by their main features like email transaction, email automation, and social media management. On March 2019, MTARGET presented at the SXSW 2019 Austin, Texas, US in the exhibition through the Bekraf (Creative Economy Agency) program.

Mentors

Farid Naufal Aslam

Farid was the CTO of a Bandung-based design studio Monoponik from 2012 to 2014, while he was still reading a degree in Business Management in Technology and Informatics at the Telkom University. In 2015, he established PasarLaut.com, an e-commerce platform for fishermen. PasarLaut.com was later incorporated to transform into a fishery tech startup Aruna. Aruna wins Gold Medalist ASEAN ICT Awards 2018 and Winner of USAID Fishackathon 2018 as well as many more local tech startups competition. Aruna has raised an undisclosed seed funding from UMG Indonesia with company valuation around IDR 70 Billion – IDR 100 Billion.

Mentors

Ogy Win

Ogy is the co-founder of Perawatku is a senior home care technology platform connecting seniors to the best quality of care on demand. The perawatku.id platform uses a proprietary matching engine that pairs seniors with the best care professionals for their needs in real-time. In 2016, Ogy, among others, 11 startups from Indonesia compete in International Startup Conference in Istanbul.
